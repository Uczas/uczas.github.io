Google Analytics

How I Use Analytics

When you visit my Website, I use Google Analytics to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behavior patterns. This is done to understand things like the number of visitors to different parts of the site.

This information is processed in a way that does not identify anyone. I do not make, and do not allow Google to make, any attempt to find out the identities of visitors.

Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy

About Cookies

Google Analytics uses its own cookies to track visitor interactions. These cookies are small text files placed on your device to collect standard internet log information. They are not used to identify you personally.

You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though this may affect site functionality.

Opt-out option: You can opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Information Sharing

I do not share, sell, or rent any personal information with third parties.
