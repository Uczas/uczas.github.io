MOUAU Job Vacancies 2025 - Academic & Non-Academic Positions

Michael Okpara University of Agriculture Umudike is recruiting for Professors, Lecturers, and Administrative Staff

Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike (MOUAU) has announced massive recruitment for the 2025/2026 academic session. The university is inviting applications from suitably qualified candidates to fill numerous teaching and non-teaching positions across various departments and colleges.

Quick Overview

Institution: Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike (MOUAU)

Positions Available: Professor, Associate Professor, Senior Lecturer, Lecturer I & II, Assistant Lecturer, Graduate Assistant, and various Non-Teaching staff

Application Method: Online via official portal

Application Deadline: Three weeks from Friday, 10th October 2025

How to Apply

Visit Application Portal

Go to the official MOUAU career portal: https://career.mouau.edu.ng

Submit Online Application

Fill out the online application form for your desired position with accurate information.

Prepare Required Documents

Have the following documents ready for upload:

  • Detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic and professional certificates
  • NYSC Discharge/Exemption Certificate
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Any other relevant documents

Print Generated CV

After submitting your application online, print a copy of the generated CV for your records.

Application Deadline

Online applications must be submitted not later than three weeks from Friday, 10th October 2025.

Late applications will not be considered. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

Important Notes

General Requirements: All applicants must possess five (5) SSCE credits including Mathematics and English Language, and must be ICT compliant. Evidence of NYSC Discharge or Exemption Certificate is mandatory for all positions.

Referees: Applicants should provide names and addresses of three referees, with at least one from their current or previous institution.

This is an excellent opportunity to join one of Nigeria's leading agricultural universities. Ensure you meet the qualifications and submit your application before the deadline.
