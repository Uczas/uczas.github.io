FUMHSK Kwale Opens Admissions for 2025/2026 Academic Session

Applications now open for UTME candidates with scores of 200 and above

The Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences (FUMHSK), Kwale, Delta State, has officially announced the commencement of its admission screening exercise for the 2025/2026 academic session. This specialized institution dedicated to healthcare education is now inviting applications from qualified UTME candidates who aspire to build careers in the medical and health sciences fields.

Official admission advertisement as published in Vanguard Newspaper, Friday, October 10, 2025

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the screening exercise, candidates must:

  • Have scored 200 and above in the 2025 UTME examinations
  • Have completed the change of institution to make FUMHSK their University of First Choice
  • Possess five (5) credit passes in relevant O'Level subjects at not more than two sittings

Available Courses

FUMHSK offers a diverse range of accredited programs across five faculties, providing students with comprehensive education in various medical and scientific disciplines:

Faculty of Medicine and Dentistry

  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.DS)
  • Bachelor of Medicine/Surgery (MBBS)

Faculty of Allied Health Sciences

  • Bachelor of Nursing Sciences (B.NSc)
  • Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Science (B.MLS)
  • B.Sc. Human Nutrition and Dietetics
  • Doctor of Physiotherapy (D.PT)

Faculty of Basic Medical Sciences

  • B.Sc. Human Anatomy
  • B.Sc. Physiology

Faculty of Pharmacy

  • Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.)

Faculty of Science

  • B.Sc. Mathematics
  • B.Sc. Physics
  • B.Sc. Chemistry
  • B.Sc. Biology
  • B.Sc. Biochemistry

General Admission Requirements

All candidates must possess five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects in the WASSCE, NECO SSCE or GCE O/L examinations at not more than two (2) sittings. Additionally, candidates must meet other matriculation requirements stipulated by JAMB.

Application Procedure

Follow these simple steps to complete your application:

Access the Portal

Visit the official university application portal at www.fumsk.edu.ng to begin your application.

Make Payment

Pay the application fee of ₦2,000 (Two Thousand Naira) using your ATM card via the Remita payment platform.

Upload Required Documents

Upload the following documents to complete your application:

  • Recent passport photograph with RED BACKGROUND
  • Original copies of credentials
  • Birth Certificate
  • Testimonials
  • Other qualifying documents

Application Deadline

The admission portal will close at 12:00 Midnight on Wednesday, October 22nd, 2025.

Late applications will not be considered, so ensure you complete and submit your application well before the deadline.
