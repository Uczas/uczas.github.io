FUMHSK Kwale Admission Portal Now Fully Functional

Applicants can now complete registration seamlessly via the official website

The Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale (FUMHSK), has announced that its admission portal is now fully operational. After weeks of development and maintenance, the university’s online application system is running smoothly, allowing prospective students to submit their applications without difficulty.

Important Notice

Applicants are strongly advised to change their institution of choice to Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale as their first choice on JAMB. Failure to do so means your records will not be forwarded by JAMB to the university for admission processing.

How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

The application process is simple and can be completed online. Follow the steps below carefully:

Visit the Official Website

Go to www.fumhsk.edu.ng and click on “Apply.”

Create or Log In to Your Account

If you already have an account, log in. Otherwise, create a new account to begin your registration.

Select Your Course

Choose your preferred course and click on “More Information.” When ready, click “Apply Now.”

Make Payment

The application fee is ₦3,000. Payment is made via Remita using your preferred method (ATM card, bank transfer, etc.). Ensure that the payer’s name matches the applicant’s details to avoid verification issues.

Complete Registration

After successful payment, you will be redirected to the application portal. Fill in your personal details, upload a recent passport photograph (with the recommended background), and attach PDF copies of your O’Level and JAMB results.

Preview and Submit

Before final submission, review all information to ensure accuracy. Once submitted, candidates will be notified of their screening schedule in due time.

Final Thoughts

The Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale, is dedicated to providing top-quality education in the health sciences. This development marks another step forward in ensuring a smooth and transparent admission process for applicants nationwide.

We wish all candidates the best of luck as they begin their journey toward a successful academic career at FUMHSK.
