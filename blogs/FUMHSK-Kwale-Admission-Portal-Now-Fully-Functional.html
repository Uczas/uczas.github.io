Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale Admission Portal Now Fully Functional

The admission portal of the Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale (FUMHSK) has now stabilized and is fully operational.

Previously, the portal was still under development, causing difficulties for applicants. However, as of the morning of Thursday, 17th, the system is now running smoothly, and applications are being processed seamlessly.

If you have been trying to apply without success in recent weeks, you are advised to revisit the school’s website to complete your registration.

Important Notice

It is mandatory to change your institution to Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale as your first choice on JAMB.

Failure to do this means your records will not be forwarded by JAMB to the university for admission consideration.

Procedures for Registration

The application process is simple and user-friendly. Kindly follow the steps below carefully:

Visit the Official Website

Go to www.fumhsk.edu.ng and click on “Apply.”

Create or Log In to Your Account

If you already have an account, simply log in. Otherwise, create a new account.

Select Course and Proceed

Select your preferred course and click “More Information.” Then click “Apply Now.” The payment details will be generated (the application fee is ₦3,000).

Update Payment Details

Update the payer’s details to match the actual bank account of the person making the payment.

Make Payment via Remita

Make your payment using any convenient method (e.g., debit card, bank transfer, etc.).

⚠️ Ensure the payer’s name matches the applicant’s details, as payments from different names may not be recognized.

Complete the Registration

Once payment is successful, you will be redirected to the application portal to continue your registration.

Upload Required Documents

Fill in all required information accurately and upload a recent passport photograph with the recommended background color.

Enter your O’Level results and upload the corresponding PDF file. Do the same for your JAMB result.

Review and Submit

Carefully review all the information you have entered before final submission.

Await Screening Notification

After submission, applicants will be notified of the screening schedule through the portal or official communication channels.

We wish all candidates the best of luck in their application process and a successful admission journey into the Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale.

May this mark the beginning of a fulfilling academic pursuit!
