Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale — Screening of UTME Candidates (2025/2026)

Screening of UTME candidates for admission into the 2025/2026 academic session.

Eligibility

Applicants must have scored 200 and above in the 2025 UTME and must have completed the change of first choice to Federal University of Medical and Health Sciences, Kwale.

Available Courses

Faculty of Medicine & Dentistry
  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.DS)
  • Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery (MBBS)
Faculty of Allied Health Sciences
  • B.Sc. Nursing Sciences (B.NSc)
  • B.Sc. Medical Laboratory Science (B.MLS)
  • B.Sc. Human Nutrition & Dietetics
  • Doctor of Physiotherapy (D.PT)
Faculty of Basic Medical Sciences
  • B.Sc. Human Anatomy
  • B.Sc. Physiology
Faculty of Pharmacy
  • Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)
Faculty of Science
  • B.Sc. Mathematics
  • B.Sc. Physics
  • B.Sc. Chemistry
  • B.Sc. Biology
  • B.Sc. Biochemistry

General Admission Requirements

Candidates must possess five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects in the WASSCE, NECO, SSCE or GCE O-Level in not more than two (2) sittings, and meet other JAMB matriculation requirements.

Procedure for Online Application

  • Apply online at: www.fumhsk.edu.ng
  • Pay the application fee of ₦2,000 via Remita (ATM card payment).
  • Upload a passport photograph with a RED background and all required credentials (birth certificate, testimonials, certificates, etc.).

Application Deadline

Closes at 12 midnight on 22nd October, 2025.

Contact

For further information call: 0807 563 3449 or 0803 234 2456.
